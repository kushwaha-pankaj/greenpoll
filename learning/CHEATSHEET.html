3 Metric Formula Box

$$IoU = \frac{\text{Area}(\text{Pred} \cap \text{GT})}{\text{Area}(\text{Pred} \cup \text{GT})}$$
$$\text{Precision} = \frac{TP}{TP + FP}, \quad \text{Recall} = \frac{TP}{TP + FN}$$
$$F_1 = 2 \cdot \frac{\text{Precision} \times \text{Recall}}{\text{Precision} + \text{Recall}}$$
mAP@50 = mean Average Precision across all classes at IoU threshold 0.50 — this is the primary paper claim.
